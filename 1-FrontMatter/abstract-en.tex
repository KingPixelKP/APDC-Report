This report describes the work carried out at \gls{GMV}, in Lisbon, Portugal,
initially within the scope of the \gls{CDA} internship associated with the
Bachelor in Computer Science at \gls{FCT}, and later consolidated in a
continuation phase directly under \gls{GMV}. The curricular internship took
place between March 11 and July 17, 2026, under the supervision of
\gls{GMV}. The work focused on the design and implementation of a C++ software
system for the programmatic control of a \gls{GNSS} signal
generator. This involved interfacing with professional \gls{SDR} hardware
through a vendor API, exposing that control through a gRPC-based architecture,
and using \gls{RS232} as the physical transport for a \gls{PPP} link carrying
IP traffic between components. The report is organised to reflect the natural
progression of the work while documenting the final architecture reached by the
project.
