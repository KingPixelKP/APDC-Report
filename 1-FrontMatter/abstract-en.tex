This report describes the work carried out during the \gls{CDA} internship
conducted at \gls{GMV}, in Lisbon, Portugal, as part of the Bachelor in
Computer Engineering at \gls{FCT}. The internship took place between March 11
and July 17, 2026, under the supervision of \gls{GMV}. The work developed
during this internship focused on the design and implementation of a C++
software system for the programmatic control of a \gls{GNSS} and \gls{SBAS} signal generator.
This involved interfacing with professional \gls{SDR} hardware through a vendor
API, designing a gRPC-based client-server architecture to expose that control
remotely, and implementing a serial communication layer over \gls{RS232} for
integration with external equipment. The report is organised to reflect the
natural progression of the work.
